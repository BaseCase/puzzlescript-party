PuzzleScript Workshop Gallery

About This Page

This is a gallery of games created during a 3-hour PuzzleScript workshop hosted at 8th Light University in September 2018 by Casey Brant. In the workshop, there was a quick intro to the PuzzleScript game engine followed by a miniature game jam.

No one at the workshop had used PuzzleScript before, and many participants had never previously made a game!
